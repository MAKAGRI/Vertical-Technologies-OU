Our Formula (10 Key Points)

  1. Discovery: Clarify goals, constraints, and success metrics.
  2. Roadmap: Prioritized milestones with owners and timelines.
  3. Data First: Instrumentation and dashboards before big bets.
  4. Pilot → Scale: Start small, prove value, then expand.
  5. Secure by Design: Access control, backups, reviews.
  6. Automation: CI/CD, alerts, and routine task offloading.
  7. Change Mgmt: Training, runbooks, and stakeholder comms.
  8. Quality: Tests, peer reviews, and SLAs/SLOs.
  9. Transparency: Weekly status, risks, and decisions logged.
  10. Handoff: Docs, workshops, and post-launch support.
